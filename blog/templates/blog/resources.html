Resources I Personally Used:

Professor Messer

Professor Messer is an awesome resource to use. He can help you prepare by providing:

  • Course Notes: Detailed notes that go over the key portions of his videos. (*You have to purchase his course notes)
  • Videos: Professor Messer provides free videos that cover each exam objective Watch his videos here.
  • Practice Exams: Professor Messer provides three different practice exams.
  • Discord Community: You can join Professor Messers discord community. The community is very supportive and members often have study sessions Click here to join his Discord community.

Darril Gibson

Darril Gibson provides clear and concise explanations in his study materials:

  • Textbook: "CompTIA Security+ Get Certified Get Ahead: SY0-601 Study Guide" is a comprehensive textbook that explains concepts clearly without being too wordy. You can buy the textbook here.
  • Practice Exams: His textbooks have a pre-assessment and post-assessment to show how much you improved while you studied. He also has practice quizzes after each topic.

Jason Dion

Jason Dion offers practice exams that simulate a real exam:

  • Practice Exams: His realistic practice exams help you simulate a real testing environment and provide you a similar format to the types of questions you might see on the exam. The exam is also timed! You can purchase the exams here. Make sure to use a promo code they're always available!

Other Resources I Used!

In addition to the resources above, I also used these resources:

  • Quizlet: Use this quizlet set to study on port numbers. Check out the port numbers quiz here.
  • Cyberkraft YouTube Channel: Comptia Security+ isn't only multiple choice. This youtube channel provides explanations on the types of performance based questions you might see on the exam. Cyberkraft YouTube channel.
  • CompTIA Security+ Exam Prep 4+ iPad App: This app was made by Thanh Hung to help you pass the SY0-601 and SY0-701 exams. Each question has explainations for the correct and incorrect answers. App Store: CompTIA Security+ Exam Prep 4+.
