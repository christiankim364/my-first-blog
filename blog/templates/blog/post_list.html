Welcome to My CompTIA Security+ Study Guide

Introduction to CompTIA Security+ 601:

CompTIA Security+ 601 is a certification that is recognized around the world. Individuals with this certification proves that you have the foundational knowledge of topics ranging in cybersecurity. It covers topics from attacks, threats, vulnerabilities, architecture & design, implementation, operations & incident response, and governance, risk and compliance.

I would say that earning a Comptia Security+ 601 certification is required for those who are looking to start a career in Information Technology security jobs. Having this certificate shows that you are ready to be hired by employers.

CompTIA Security+ 601 is Expiring Soon!

The Comptia Security+ 601 exam will expire on July 31st, 2024. If you are haven't started studying yet I would recommend taking the Comptia Security+ 701 exam instead since it will last longer.

Why I recommend earning this certificate

CompTIA Security+ is crucial for several reasons:

  • Industry Recognition: Certifications are recognized by employers, allowing you to prove knowledge of cybersecurity.
  • Obtaining a job: Getting this certificate will look good on your resume and show employers that you are qualified.
  • Continuous Learning: Information Technology and cybersecurity are fields that continuously evolve and grow. Earning certificates like security+ will make you learn about new advancements.

About CompTIA

CompTIA stands for "The Computing Technology Industry Association". They provide certificates ranging from A+, Network+, Security+, Cloud+, Linux+, Server+, etc. These certificates are all well respected and help validate the skills of those who hold them.
